## My Favorite Word
_serendipity_
## Why I chose this word & its personal meaning
I chose _serendipity_ because it reminds me that many good things in life happen unexpectedly. To me, the word represents the joy of discovering something wonderful without searching for it. It encourages me to stay open-minded and appreciate the surprises that life brings.
## Dictionary definition
_Serendipity_ (noun): the occurrence of fortunate or pleasant discoveries by accident.
## Original sentence
Last week, I experienced true serendipity when I found an old friend in a coffee shop I had randomly decided to visit.
